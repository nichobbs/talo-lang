# A new president

- **Register:** hard news (politics)
- **Source:** original simplified item, BBC-style (not a verbatim reproduction)
- **Proper nouns:** none
- **Exercises:** `pelesiden` president + `menteli` minister, an **ordinal**
  (`mope` = third), the genitive `we`, benefactive `na`, `mungi` approximation,
  a date, reported speech.

## English

The people chose a new president on Sunday. She is the country's third president.
The minister said: we work for all people. About a thousand people came.

## Talo

```talo
# 1 — pili "choose"; new president = pelesidenka nayape; date dinka ta
hitoka pilito pelesidenka nayape dinka ta     › The people chose a new president on Sunday.
# gloss: person-N choose-V president-N new-MOD day-N one

# 2 — genitive: POSSESSOR we HEAD (0005); ordinal mope = three+-pe = "third"
te yato negalaka we pelesidenka mope          › She is the country's third president.
# gloss: it COP country-N GEN president-N third

# 3–4 — reported speech; na = benefactive ("for"), postposed
mentelika semato li                            › The minister said:
# gloss: minister-N say-V COMPLETIVE
mi kasito hitoka ote na                        › we work for all people.
# gloss: we work-V person-N all for

# 5 — mungi before the numeral; sebu = thousand
hitoka mungi sebu datanto li                   › About a thousand people came.
# gloss: person-N about thousand come-V COMPLETIVE
```
