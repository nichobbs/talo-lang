# A baby at the zoo

- **Register:** soft news (nature)
- **Source:** original simplified item, BBC-style (not a verbatim reproduction)
- **Proper nouns:** none
- **Exercises:** `lahili` be-born, `tokea` appear, the compound `dobukebunka`
  (zoo = animal-garden), an **ordinal** (`tape` = first, `NUM + -pe`), a date.

## English

A baby was born at the zoo on Sunday. It is the first baby this year. The small
animal appeared in the morning. Many people came. Children watch the animal.

## Talo

```talo
# 1 — lahili "be born"; dobukebunka "zoo" (compound, COMP-033); date dinka ta
bayika lahilito li dobukebunka lo dinka ta    › A baby was born at the zoo on Sunday.
# gloss: baby-N be.born-V COMPLETIVE zoo-N at day-N one

# 2 — ordinal: tape = one+-pe = "first" (0010 §2.1); taunka ini = this year
te yato bayika tape taunka ini lo             › It is the first baby this year.
# gloss: it COP baby-N first year-N this at

# 3 — tokea "appear"; asagohanka = morning (a noun, takes -ka)
dobuka pikope tokeato li asagohanka lo        › The small animal appeared in the morning.
# gloss: animal-N small-MOD appear-V COMPLETIVE morning-N at

# 4–5
hitoka ingi datanto li                        › Many people came.
# gloss: person-N many come-V COMPLETIVE
totoka pu miluto dobuka                        › Children watch the animal.
# gloss: child-N PL watch-V animal-N
```
