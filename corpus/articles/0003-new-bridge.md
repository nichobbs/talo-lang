# A new bridge opens

- **Register:** local news (built environment)
- **Source:** original simplified item, BBC-style (not a verbatim reproduction)
- **Proper nouns:** none

## English

A new bridge opened in the city today. It is very long. Now people go from the
house to the garden quickly. The chief said: this bridge is good.
