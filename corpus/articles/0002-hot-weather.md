# A very hot day

- **Register:** soft news (weather)
- **Source:** original simplified item, BBC-style (not a verbatim reproduction)
- **Proper nouns:** none

## English

The weather is very hot today. The sun is big and there is no wind. People
drink much water. Doctors say: stay in the house.

## Talo

```talo

# gloss: you be.at-V house-N at
```
