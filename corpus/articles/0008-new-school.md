# A new school opens

- **Register:** local news (education)
- **Source:** original simplified item, BBC-style (not a verbatim reproduction)
- **Proper nouns:** none
- **Exercises (longer piece):** the compound `belayabaitika` (school =
  learn-house), the **agent derivation** `ayalikika` (teacher = teach + `-ki`),
  an **ordinal** (`dinka tape` = the first day), a **conditional** with `toki`
  (`fi … , toki …`), a **yes/no question** (`ke`), the quantifier `kila` (each),
  and benefactive/dative `na`.

## English

A new school opened today. Many children came on the first day. The teacher said:
welcome! The children read books and write. Are the children happy? Yes. If a
child learns, then she knows more. The teacher gives each child a book. The school
is big and new. Now the city has a good school.

## Talo

```talo
# 1 — belayabaitika "school" (compound, COMP-010); leo = today
belayabaitika nayape bukato li leo               › A new school opened today.
# gloss: school-N new-MOD open-V COMPLETIVE today

# 2 — ordinal: dinka tape = "the first day" (day-N + one+-pe)
totoka pu ingi datanto li dinka tape             › Many children came on the first day.
# gloss: child-N PL many come-V COMPLETIVE day-N first

# 3 — ayalikika "teacher" (teach + -ki agent + -ka); kalibu = welcome (phatic)
ayalikika semato li                              › The teacher said:
# gloss: teacher-N say-V COMPLETIVE

# 4 — two verbs coordinated with i "and"
totoka pu bacato honka i tulito                  › The children read books and write.
# gloss: child-N PL read-V book-N and write-V

# 5 — yes/no question with clause-final ke; answered hi "yes"
totoka pu yato senanpe ke                         › Are the children happy?
# gloss: child-N PL COP happy-MOD Q

# 6 — conditional with explicit toki "then" heading the result (0005 §1)
fi totoka belayato, toki te tauto lebi           › If a child learns, then she knows more.
# gloss: if child-N learn-V, then it know-V more

# 7 — kila "each" determiner; na = dative/benefactive recipient
ayalikika donato honka kila totoka na            › The teacher gives each child a book.
# gloss: teacher-N give-V book-N each child-N to

# 8 — two predicate modifiers joined by i
belayabaitika yato gandepe i nayape              › The school is big and new.
# gloss: school-N COP big-MOD and new-MOD

# 9 — existential/locative kuna; inino = now
inino belayabaitika haope kunato kotaka lo       › Now the city has a good school.
# gloss: now school-N good-MOD exist-V city-N at
```
