# A phone that thinks

- **Register:** modern technology news
- **Source:** original factual summary of real developments (on-device AI phone
  features, mid-2026) written for this corpus — **not** a reproduction of any
  outlet's text.
- **Proper nouns:** none (generic — "a big company").
- **Exercises:** the 0012 grammar (complementiser `ce`, comparative `fe`, serial
  modality, `-pe` relatives) and `hoka` "other"; rich existing tech vocabulary.

## English

A big company announced new programs for phones. The phones use artificial
intelligence. Now a phone can warn about a fake call. It can find a picture
quickly. People can send photos to other phones. The company says that the phone
protects your data. Some people think that a smart phone is very useful. But
others fear that the machines know too much. A smart phone is better than an old
phone. The company should block fake calls.

## Talo

```talo
# tech vocab: gulamu program, dewa phone, inteliyena intelligence/AI
kaisaka anunioto gulamuka nayape dewaka na                  › A big company announced new programs for phones.
# gloss: company-N announce-V program-N new-MOD phone-N for
dewaka pakaito inteliyenaka                                 › The phones use artificial intelligence.
# gloss: phone-N use-V intelligence-N

# serial modality (bisa "can"); "fake call" = yobuka salape (wrong call)
inino dewaka bisato keikokuto yobuka salape                 › Now a phone can warn about a fake call.
# gloss: now phone-N can-V warn-V call-N fake-MOD
te bisato pataito picaka hayaipe                            › It can find a picture quickly.
# gloss: it can-V find-V picture-N fast-MOD

# hoka "other" (postposed determiner) + role marker on the whole NP
hitoka bisato kiliato fotoka dewaka hoka su                 › People can send photos to other phones.
# gloss: person-N can-V send-V photo-N phone-N other toward

# complementiser ce; protect = salamata (safe + -ta causative)
kaisaka semato ce dewaka salamatato yu we datoka            › The company says that the phone protects your data.
# gloss: company-N say-V that phone-N protect-V you GEN data-N

# ce embedding; "smart" = inteliyenape, "useful" = pakaipe
hitoka bala omouto ce dewaka inteliyenape yato pakaipe sana › Some people think that a smart phone is very useful.
# gloss: person-N some think-V that phone-N smart-MOD COP useful-MOD very
ma hitoka hoka takuto ce kikaika tauto ingi sana            › But others fear that the machines know too much.
# gloss: but person-N other fear-V that machine-N know-V much very

# comparison with fe "than" (0012 §1)
dewaka inteliyenape yato haope lebi dewaka tuape fe         › A smart phone is better than an old phone.
# gloss: phone-N smart-MOD COP good-MOD more phone-N old-MOD than

# serial modality (beki "should") + katasa "block/forbid"
kaisaka bekito katasato yobuka salape                       › The company should block fake calls.
# gloss: company-N should-V forbid-V call-N fake-MOD
```
