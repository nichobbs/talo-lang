Talo

A small, regular, no-exceptions auxiliary language — built so your first foreign language can be an easy one.

Ahoi! Mi yato Milaka.
Hello! I am Mila.

Fi panika kunato, mi pinato.
If there is water, I drink.

Why Talo is easy

  • Every letter has one sound, and stress is always on the first syllable — read any word correctly on sight.
  • No conjugations, genders, or irregular forms. A word's job is shown by a one-syllable ending you can always see.
  • Build new words from pieces you know — teacher = teach + "one who", school = learn + "place".
  • Words you half-recognise already: drawn from many of the world's largest languages, no single one dominating.
